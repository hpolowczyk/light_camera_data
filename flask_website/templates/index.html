Lights! Camera! Data! is a data visualization project based on 250 of the highest grossing movies of all time according to the amount they grossed over their opening weekend.


<<<<<<< HEAD

It delves into the connections between films, actors and the rating system using a chord diagram. It investigates foreign gross box office success via map.

It explores the total gross earnings per studio, broken down by movie, through an exploding bubble chart. It showcases genre themes and uses size to display financial success.
